About the Author

Becky Isjwara is a writer, strategist, and creator who's spent the past three years building a creative life on top of a 9-to-5. She has professional experience as a ghostwriter, editor, and reporter and has published hundreds of pieces across YouTube, Substack, and beyond. Bite-Sized Creativity is her guide for anyone who wants to do the same without burning out or waiting for permission. She has a Notion template for everything and spends weekends chasing light with her film camera.
